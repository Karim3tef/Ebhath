About Us

Non-profit Organization

Ebhath is a fiscally sponsored official 501(c)3 nonprofit dedicated to closing the educational gap in scientific research resources by offering courses in underrepresented languages for underrepresented minorities. In 2023, Ebhath began working to bridge this gap in the Arabic language, and we are currently expanding our efforts to include Filipino and Swahili. We are currently recruiting mentors to develop courses in Portuguese and Indian languages, although translation and recording in these languages have not yet commenced. Shortly, we will launch a research program in Egypt that teaches scientific research in the native Arabic language of the country. Despite the existence of a few highly selective and experienced national and international research programs, there is a significant void where no free, fully accessible research program teaches in the native language. Our aim is to establish a presence in more countries by developing local chapters to offer similar programming in their native tongues. All of this is part of our aim of "closing others' people's problems as we developed a solution for our own problem." This is what every member, whether staff or student, at Ebhath is taking to heart and making a core part of their mission.

About Us

Research Institution

Ebhath, a fiscally sponsored 501(c)3 nonprofit, aims to close the educational gap in scientific research by offering courses in underrepresented languages. Starting with Arabic in 2023, we are expanding to Filipino and Swahili and recruiting mentors for Portuguese and Indian languages. Soon, we will launch a research program in Egypt teaching scientific research in Arabic, addressing the lack of free, accessible programs in the native language.

Our mission is to develop local chapters in more countries, providing similar programming in native languages, embodying our commitment to solving others' problems as we solved our own.
